info

Tomi Abe is a strategic creative and digital leader working at the intersection of design, technology, media, and research.

Over the past decade, he has partnered with early-stage ventures, growth-stage companies, and established institutions, including YC-backed startups, Fortune 500 ecosystems, and impact-driven organizations across emerging and established markets, building systems that help teams communicate clearly, ship confidently, and scale with coherence.

Beyond client work, he speaks, trains, and mentors on emerging technologies, systems thinking, and responsible innovation. With foundations in computer science, design, and creative technology, and experience spanning in-house, agency, and consulting environments, he believes knowledge should empower, not overwhelm.

Speaking, Workshops & Mentorship

Tomi designs and delivers talks, workshops, and mentorship programs at the intersection of design, emerging technologies, systems thinking, and creative entrepreneurship, helping teams and individuals apply these tools in practical, responsible, and strategically meaningful ways.

His audiences span early-stage founders, creative professionals, corporate teams, and global organizations across Africa and beyond. He is known for translating complex technical concepts into clear frameworks that support better decision-making, communication, and execution.
